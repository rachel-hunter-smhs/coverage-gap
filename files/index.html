National uninsured rate · 2010–2023

Uninsured rate fell from 18% in 2010 to 9.6% in 2023.

The sharp drop in 2014 reflects the ACA Marketplace and Medicaid expansion taking effect. The 2020 uptick coincides with pandemic-era job losses, followed by recovery through expanded subsidies and continuous Medicaid enrollment.
